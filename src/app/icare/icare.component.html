i-Care
A lot of children belonging to the fishing community in Mangalore drop out of school to help their parents in their daily chores.
Beach school tries to reduce this student drop out by instilling in them, an interest towards practical science, mathematics & astronomy.
Volunteers for i-Care teach extra curricular activities like origami, dance to differently-abled children in Lion's School, Surathkal.
We plan to have many more such uplifting i-Care activities in the future.
